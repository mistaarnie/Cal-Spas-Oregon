    Patio™ Spa Series

    The Patio™ Series is divided into two categories: Patio™ and Patio™ Plus. Just like its name, the Patio™ series are patio™ size spas. The main differences are Patio™ Spas are 110 plug-n-play spas that feature jet packages from 11 to 22 jets. Patio™ Plus Spas are 220 volts, 5 BHP that feature jet packages from 25 to 32 jets.

    Escape™ Spa Series

    Escape™ Spa Series

    The Platinum™ Series still is one of our most powerful spa series with standard features like the Adjustable Therapy System™, 2 and 3 pump configurations, 24/7 circulation pump, essentially making the 60 jets a 4 pump spa and the 80 jets a 5 pump spa. Also standard in the Platinum™ Series is our Hybrid Clear Water System and 150 sq. ft. BioClean Filters™ which provides more clear, clean water while lowering maintenance.
